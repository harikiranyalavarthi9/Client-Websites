Guest Reviews


Pleasantly surprised

My wife and I have made a few trips to this area and normally stay at the run of the mill cookie cutter hotels. This time I search around for something different. In my search I came across this B&B. We had never stayed at one before. This was a new experience. I had heard good and bad about B&B's so we were a little hesitant not knowing what to expect. From the moment we walked in and greeted by Anne, we felt very comfortable. We were very pleasantly surprised with the great accommodations, delicious breakfast, and the overall wonderful hospitality shown by Anne and Steve. We will be definitely be staying here again!! We also know now the bar is set high for the next B&B we try. I couldn't say enough wonderful things about this place!! Thanks Anne and Steve you made our trip fabulous!!

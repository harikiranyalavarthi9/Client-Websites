Directions & Location

Just minutes from these popular Central New York attractions
Destiny USA
Syracuse University
SUNY Oswego
The New York State Fair
The Carrier Dome, home of the Syracuse Orangemen!
Finger Lakes Wineries

From the North:

Take 81 South to Exit 30. Turn right on Route 31. Go 5 miles to Euclid (Intersection of Rt. 31 & Morgan Road) Turn right at the light onto Morgan Road. Mansion is 1.5 miles on the left.

From the South:

Take 81 North to Exit 29. North on 481 to Exit 12. Turn right on Rt. 31. Go 1 mile to Euclid, left on Morgan Road. Go 1.5 miles, Mansion on the left.

From the West:

Take Thruway East to Exit 39. Take 690 East to John Glen Blvd. to its end at Buckly Road. Turn right, take first left which is Morgan Road. Go 4.5 miles, cross Oneida River Bridge, Mansion is first home on the left.

From the East:

Take Thruway West to Exit 34A. Take 481 North to Exit 12. Turn right on Rt.31. Go 1 mile to Euclid. Left on Morgan Road. Mansion is 1.5 miles on left.

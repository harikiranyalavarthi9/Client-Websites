Breakfast

Breakfast at the Mansion is served in our elegant, cherry ceiling dining room, next to a cozy, hand carved mahogany fireplace, and consists of three courses ~ fresh baked homemade muffins or other pastries, a seasonal fruit dish, and a hot entree. Freshly brewed coffee and a selection of juices and teas complement the meal. This is graciously served on place settings of fine china, silver, and crystal in an intimate candlelight setting. Special diets are no problem as long as you tell us in advance. Gluten-free, vegetarian, vegan and paleo diets diets are happily accommodated.

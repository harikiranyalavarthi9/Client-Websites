Welcome to Bella Tours & Travels

Our country is a magnificent place with countless wonderful cities to see and explore. We offer customized groups tours that provide a safe and affordable way to experience the sights and sounds of some of the most interesting places in the world.

Our Services

Located in Liverpool - New York, our group travel services are perfect field trips for students, senior citizen tours, and corporate traveling.

School Tours

Our company arranges a variety of field trips, from 1-day outings to multi-day tours. We always plan our school field trips to be both educational and fun, and we work closely with the school districts to ensure we follow the correct policies. Allow us to arrange your next class trip at your school, and we will handle everything including hotel accommodations, transportation, activities, and meals.

Senior Citizen Tour

Enjoy your golden years with a completely customized tour that handles all the necessary accommodations. We plan single and multi-day trips based on the tour group's personal interests and budgetary needs. Every part of our senior citizen tours are handicap accessible.

Open Tours

This is a great way to visit the places you have always wanted to see without breaking your bank account. We arrange different tours on a monthly basis that are open for the public to join. Every year the trips are different, and some of the most popular destinations include New York City, Seattle, Branson Missouri, and other destinations in the USA and Canada. Open tours are available for people of all ages. One Day & Multi-Day Trips Available.

Corporate Travel

When you and your co-workers or employees are planning a business trip, allow Bella Tours & Travel to handle all the arrangements. Whether it is for business or leisure, we will take care of the hotels, meals, and events. We can also arrange great group rates for motor coach, trains, or airplane transportation.

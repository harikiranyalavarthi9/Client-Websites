    Area Attractions

    There are some great attractions in our area. If you like shopping, we are 2 minutes from the Great Northern Mall and 12 minutes from Destiny Mall. There are also many antique stores in the area. If you like boating, fishing, or swimming, we have a private dock on the Oneida River with access to Oneida Lake, the Finger Lakes, and Lake Ontario. For bike and snowmobile fans, we are 4 miles from the Oswego County trail system with over one hundred miles of trails! We are just 15 minutes from downtown Syracuse with museums, shows, the Burnet Park Zoo, many fine restaurants, and Syracuse University. We are also within a short drive from several state and county parks, the New York State Fairgrounds, and dozens of wineries.

    • Cruise the Erie Canal on our Riverboat

    Enjoy the magical late afternoon and early evening rays of sun on the Oneida River aboard our canal boat "ELLA C". Cross a trip through a lock on the Erie Canal off your bucket list! Captain Steve will keep you laughing and your glass full as he skillfully navigates the still waters. Ask about a dinner cruise! We might start with grilled shrimp kabobs, a lovely Caprice or Caesar salad, salmon, chicken or steak grilled to your order, and Central New Yorks famous salt potatoes along with locally grown vegetables. We'll finish up with desert in the gazebo, or you can take it to your room to savor later.



    Kayaking

    Kayaks are available for rent just down the road in Phoenix, or you may borrow ours. Try out our kayak launch! You'll see geese and their many goslings, ducks, blue heron, fox, beaver and even bald eagles on the Oneida River.

    Birding

    For thousands of years, migrating hawks, eagles, raptors and songbirds have flocked along the southeastern shores of Lake Ontario, only a short drive from the Mansion. These migratory routes provide ample opportunities for birders to witness unusual or record sightings. Many species of birds can be found along the Oswego Canal, including the elusive Osprey and Bald Eagle. Derby Hill, designated a New York State "Important Bird Area", counted a world-record 19,531 red-tailed hawks. The Montezuma Wildlife Refuge and Montezuma Audubon Center are less than an hour away.

    Snowmobiling

    Bring your snow machine! A trail runs by River Edge Mansion, connecting to miles and miles of trails. Thanks to the area's lake effect snowfall and expansive trail system of over 360 miles of groomed paths, Oswego County offers the ideal conditions for optimum snowmobiling.

    Wine and Cheese Trails

    It's a New York State of wineries, breweries, distilleries and meaderies, we have them all in Central New York. With more than 100 wineries in the Finger Lakes, it would take a week to visit them all, but you can take a day and visit one of the wine trails; the Keuka Lake Wine Trail, the Canandaigua Wine Trail, or our favorite, the Seneca Lake Wine Trail. The Finger Lakes is home to a new cheese trail to accompany all that wine. Or go north and try the Thousand Island Wine Trail. Pack the B&B with your friends and take a limo to the wine tastings ... we'll be happy to make you a reservation.
    Did you know you can make your own wine ... a short drive away is Lakeland Winery. At this casual winery you will learn about wine making through the ages, be provided with several different wine to taste before selecting the wine you will make. Seven or eight weeks later, you'll come back to cork, bottle and label your wine to take home. Each batch makes 29 - 30 bottles of one style.



    Fishing

    The Mansion is located on the Oneida River, approximately 5 miles from lock 23 and Oneida Lake. Large mouth and small mouth bass, walleye, tiger musky and northern pike are typical, Pan fish found in the river are black crappie, yellow perch, bluegill, white perch and brown bullhead. Other fish are channel catfish, carp and sheephead. A free boat launch is about 3 miles downriver and there is no charge for docking for guests staying at the Mansion. There is plenty of room for your boat trailer.

    Shopping is Plentiful

    River Edge Mansion is a mile and a half from the Great Northern Mall and 15 minutes from the fourth largest mall in the States, DestiNY USA! The Waterloo Premium Outlets, with over 100 outlet stores is less than an hour away. Antique shops abound in Syracuse and the surrounding areas. The Syracuse Antiques Exchange has four floors of fabulous antiques.

    New York State Fairgrounds

    There's more than the State Fair at the Fairgrounds. The Fairground hosts over 100 incredible non-fair events throughout the year such as the Syracuse Nationals, the Salt City Antique Show, and RV, train and boat shows.

    Walking and Biking

    The roads surrounding the Mansion and along the canal offer perfect biking opportunities. The Oswego County recreation trail offers miles of hiking on a reclaimed abandoned railroad bed that winds through picturesque Central New York farmland, fields and villages.

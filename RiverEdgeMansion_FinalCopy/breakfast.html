    Gracious Service

    Breakfast at the Mansion is served in our elegant cherry ceiling dining room, next to a cozy hand carved mahogany fireplace and is brought to you in three courses – fresh baked breads, muffins or pastries, a seasonal fruit dish, and a hot entrée. Freshly brewed coffee and a selection of juices and teas complement the meal. You will be graciously served on place settings of fine china, silver and crystal in an intimate candlelight setting.

    Organic foods and free range chicken eggs are utilized wherever possible. Special diets are no problem as long as you tell us in advance. Gluten-free, vegetarian and other dietary restrictions are happily accommodated with advance notice.

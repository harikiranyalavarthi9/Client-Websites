    Enjoy a Perfect Getaway

    Tastefully decorated rooms create an atmosphere of elegance and refinement yet allow you to completely relax and feel right at home. Languish in a whirlpool bath, read by a marble fireplace or dream under a canopy bed. Spacious surroundings inside and out give you an opportunity for complete privacy or gracious sharing. A private pathway leads you directly to the Oneida River where you will find private docking facilities for boating, fishing and swimming.

    Fluffy robes, blow dryers, shampoos, shower gels and lotions are provided in each room to make your stay relaxing and comfortable and your packing easier. Wi-Fi is available throughout the B&B along with a nice selection of books and magazines along with a TV in each guest room. The Mansion has both central air-conditioning and romantic fireplaces. A microwave, refrigerator and Keurig coffee maker allow you to make yourself at home. A complimentary glass of wine and a charcuterie board, or coffee and tea and sweet treat is offered upon arrival.


    Oneida River Room

    Premium - $200/Night

    A truly magical experience! This enchanting room will fulfill all your romantic dreams, with its architectural details and original moldings, king canopy bed, two-person whirlpool tub, shower and marble fireplace. Relax on your private balcony, overlooking the Erie Canal, while enjoying a complimentary beverage. Unwind in front of a cozy fire while warm waters swirl around you. Rest in comfort on the pillow top mattress and feel totally refreshed. This luxurious room also features antiques, oriental rugs, handmade interior shutters, TV/DVD and a private entrance. If you choose the Oneida River room, a coffee tray left just outside your door will brighten your early morning. Priced at $200 per night.



    Seneca River Room

    Popular - $175/Night

    Enjoy lazy mornings in the Seneca River room watching yachts and riverboats float by. Experience this large, bright room with a king size antique bed, decorated with oriental rugs, lovely antique furniture and fresh cut flowers for that special touch. Glance out your windows and enjoy the beautiful view, overlooking the Oneida River. This upscale room also features a large private bathroom with a two- person whirlpool tub and separate shower. In room TV-VCR for your use. Priced at $175 per night.



    Oswego River Room

    Red Hot - $150/Night

    This smaller, but delightful room has a queen antique bed with a dreamy pillow top mattress. Nicely decorated with antiques, this room is perfectly priced for a spontaneous weekend getaway or for the weekday business traveler. A large private bathroom with a whirlpool tub and shower is attached. There is a TV in the room for your enjoyment. Priced at $150.00 per night.




    Onondaga River Room

    Famous - $180/Night

    This spacious and airy room on the first floor features both a queen bed and a double bed. This room was originally the formal parlor in the Mansion and features a marble fireplace, beautifully carved door frames and moldings, interior shutters and a large private bath. Unwind with a book and beverage in front of the fire or watch your favorite TV program in the wingback chairs. The front porch is just steps away, where you can relax and ponder how life might have been like in the 1800s. This room is perfect for guests who don’t care for stairs or who opt for separate beds. Priced at $180 per night.

    Culinary Weekend

    Schedule a Culinary weekend with your friends or siblings!
    Friday afternoon, guests arrive at the Mansion at their leisure and check in. At 5 pm Captain Steve will host a reception with champagne and a stationary display of local cheeses in the Mansion's dining room. Each participant will be presented with an embroidered apron and a group picture will be taken. Afterwards you will enjoy a limousine ride to Mohegan Manor for dinner. Saturday morning at the River Edge you’ll enjoy a specially cooked three course gourmet breakfast of fresh fruits, croissants or scones and cooked to order farm fresh egg omelets or waffles with New York maple syrup and bacon, sausage or ham. Saturday morning from about 10:30 until noon guests will accompany the cook to the CNY Regional Famers Market to choose items for the menu for that evening’s dinner and Sunday's farm to table breakfast.

    The seasonal menu will emphasize farm to table ingredients, such as local cheeses, fresh eggs, fruits, vegetables and NYS wines.

    After the farmers market, you may elect to visit nearby Destiny USA mall or the Syracuse Antiques Exchange or visit downtown Syracuse for lunch. Guests will return to the Mansion and gather for wine at 5 and to complete dinner preparations. At 7:30 pm guests will dine with the cook and Captain Steve and enjoy the meal you have helped prepare. Guests gather for breakfast prepared from ingredients chosen at the farmers market. The cook will demonstrate eggs Benedict with wilted spinach. Here you will have the opportunity to learn how to make hollandaise sauce and poached eggs. Breakfast will include fruits and goodies from the Market, juices, coffee and tea.

    The culinary weekend will be limited to 3 couples. The cost including two nights lodging at River Edge Mansion, reception, three meals, hands-on group cooking and visit to the Regional Market, is $700 per couple (or less, depending upon your room) including all taxes and gratuities.
